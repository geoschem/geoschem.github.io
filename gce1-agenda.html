Agenda

GCE1 was held virtually via Zoom on 1-2 September 2020 and featured keynote presentations by Prof. Nadine Unger at University of Exeter, Dr Anja Schmidt at University of Cambridge, and Dr Folkert Boersma at KNMI/Wageningen University.
