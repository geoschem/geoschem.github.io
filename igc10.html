Overview

The 10th International GEOS-Chem Meeting (IGC10) was held at Washington University in St. Louis (Missouri, USA) on June 7–10, 2022 with 272 registrants from 103 institutions and 17 countries. Model clinics were held on June 6, 2022. Follow the links for:

  • Oral and poster presentations from the meeting;
  • Model development priority list coming out of the meeting.

We are grateful for the financial support for the meeting offered by NASA, NSF, NOAA, EPRI, and Washington University in St. Louis, James McKelvey School of Engineering..

We look forward to seeing you at the 11th International GEOS-Chem Meeting.
