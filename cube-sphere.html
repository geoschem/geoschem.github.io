Cubed-sphere grid illustrations

GEOS-Chem "HP" (aka GCHP) represents the next generation of GEOS-Chem with a distributed memory capability designed to enable efficient scaling across many cores, and thus enable finer resolution simulations. GCHP also has the advantage of using a "cubed-sphere" geometry that will enable more accurate transport and eliminate the polar singularity inherent to lat-lon grids.
