Overview

The charge of the GEOS-Chem Steering Committee (GCSC) is to:

  1. Develop long-term strategies for the model and for the functioning of the user community
  2. Prioritize and enable model development in consultation with the GEOS-Chem Working Groups (WGs)
  3. Review model benchmarks and monitor the integrity and traceability of the model.

GEOS-Chem Steering Committee membership is rotating and international. Members are personally invested in GEOS-Chem through their research. Current members are:
