The 2nd GEOS-Chem Asia meeting (GCA2) will be held May 19-21, 2025 at NUIST. Please check back later for more information.

GEOS-Chem is a global/regional model of atmospheric composition used by a large research community worldwide for a very wide range of applications. GEOS-Chem Asia meetings are organized by the Joint NUIST-Harvard Laboratory for Air Quality and Climate (JLAQC) to benefit the Asian community using GEOS-Chem and to supplement the International GEOS-Chem meetings (IGC) held in alternate years. See the Presentations and Posters from the GCA1 meeting held at NUIST in May 2018.

For questions regarding the meeting please contact Prof. Hong Liao (NUIST, GEOS-Chem Steering Committee member and local host), Prof. Ke Li (NUIST, local organizer), or Daniel Jacob (Harvard, GEOS-Chem Co-Model Scientist).

We look forward to seeing you in Nanjing!
