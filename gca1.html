The First Regional GEOS-Chem Asia Meeting (GCA1) was held at Nanjing University of Information Science & Technology (NUIST) on May 21-23, 2018 with 115 participants from 26 institutions and 7 countries. Follow the links for:

  • Oral and poster presentations from the meeting;
  • GCA1 participants list and contact information.

The meeting was organized by the NUIST-Harvard Joint Laboratory for Air Quality and Climate (JLAQC).

We look forward to seeing you at the 2nd GEOS-Chem Asia meeting at NUIST in May 2020, and before that at the 9th International GEOS-Chem Meeting at Harvard on May 1-4, 2019!
