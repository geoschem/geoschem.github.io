Keynote Speakers

GCE3 featured keynote talks by researchers representing modelling communities such as NAME, AQUM, WRF-Chem, EMEP4UK and UKCA. Speakers included Lucy Neal from the UK MetOffice, Alex Archibald from University of Cambridge, Massimo Vieno from the UK Centre for Ecology and Hydrology and Clare Heaviside from University College London.
